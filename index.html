About


My name is Daniel Hector, i am a self-taught artist. my interest in art started at the age of 6 when I first laid eyes on my brother's artworks. He was "naturally talented",
whereas I started out tracing before I dived into freehand drawing. Through the years my skills gradually improved with accuracy and realism. I have dabbled in many mediums i.e. pencil drawing, painting
(acrylic, oil & gouache paints), clay sculpting, wood carving and digital drawing/painting.
